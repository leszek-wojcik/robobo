uint32_t data_frame_opt_t::ul_fsedge

Frame Sync. edge detection. For receiver configuration, SSC_RFMR_FSEDGE_POSITIVE or SSC_RFMR_FSEDGE_NEGATIVE. For transmitter configuration, SSC_TFMR_FSEDGE_POSITIVE or SSC_TFMR_FSEDGE_NEGATIVE.

uint32_t data_frame_opt_t::ul_fsos

Frame Sync. output selection. For receiver configuration, one of SSC_RFMR_FSOS_NONE, SSC_RFMR_FSOS_NEGATIVE, SSC_RFMR_FSOS_POSITIVE, SSC_RFMR_FSOS_LOW, SSC_RFMR_FSOS_HIGH or SSC_RFMR_FSOS_TOGGLING. For transmitter configuration, one of SSC_TFMR_FSOS_NONE, SSC_TFMR_FSOS_NEGATIVE, SSC_TFMR_FSOS_POSITIVE SSC_TFMR_FSOS_LOW, SSC_TFMR_FSOS_HIGH, SSC_TFMR_FSOS_TOGGLING,

uint32_t data_frame_opt_t::ul_msbf

Bit sequence LSBF or MSBF. For receiver configuration, SSC_RFMR_MSBF or 0. For transmitter configuration, SSC_TFMR_MSBF or 0.
